About Us

The Serampore Mission of the Baptist Missionary Society was established on 10th January 1800, when William Carey had already spent about seven years in North Bengal.

What is Our Goal?

Aim to collaborate & develop course with a centric course having opportunities nationally & Globally in the corporate research & in industrial Sector. The supreme task of this Historic institution is to impart to our students knowledge that shall culminate in true wisdom, the sure foundation both of well-being and well-doing. The College stands for education of the whole person enabling our students to people of sound learning and sterling character and motivating them to serve humanity with dedication. The College affirms from experience that the intermingling of students from different backgrounds is healthy and is of great help in the interest of broad culture and strong character.

How does this seminar help?

This seminar helps the students to gain better insights on the career path and to get a birds eye view in a holistic view covering public Health & professional Pest Control.

How can Vector Control good career growth?

Vector Control offers a dynamic and evolving career path with opportunities for growth, research, policy influence, and societal impact. Professionals who stay informed, embrace interdisciplinary approaches, and contribute to advancements in the field are well-positioned for long-term career success.
