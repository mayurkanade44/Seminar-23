About Serampore

The Serampore College was established in 1800, under the ages of a visionary person Mr. William Carey had already spent about seven years in North Bengal. The college aims to collaborate & develop new sustainable course having employment and career pursuing opportunities nationally & globally in corporate research, industrial Sector creating a sure foundation both of well-being and well-doing.

About Smart Connect

SMART CONNECT is an initiative by Mr. Stelson Quadros, a second generation entrepreneur, who fiercely believes change must be initiated through discussion and sharing knowledge, opportunities and evolving is the only best way ahead for society & nation. His long term vision, for industry development by collaborative and sustainable opportunity creation makes him one of a kind dynamic contributor in the Research & Academia fraternity and Indian Professional Pest Management Industry.

Smart Connect is one such initiative to establish a dependable exchange platform.

  • To promote industry related knowledge sharing.
  • Research paper discussion.
  • Policy makers and industry interactions.
  • Industry peer to peer meets.
  • Industry promotion.
  • Conclaves to discuss & strengthen the future of INDIAN [MSME] business offering.

FAQs

How does this seminar help?

This seminar helps the students to gain better insights on the career path and to get a birds eye view in a holistic view covering public Health & professional Pest Control.

How can Vector Control be a good career growth?

Vector Control offers a robust, dynamic and evolving career path with opportunities in research, policy influence, and societal impact. Professionals who stay informed, embrace interdisciplinary approaches, and contribute to advancements in the field are well-positioned for long-term career success. Multiple opportunities exist on a national and global format in Hospitality, Food industry & good manufacturer sector and in public sector.

How do i connect to the speaker?

Each participant attending the seminar can take notes and share their views or questions over email or connect to the speaker over other social media platform as provided in their profile details.
